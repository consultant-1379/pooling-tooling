Introduction

Resource Pooling Tool (RPT) is a tool which was created with the initial scope of efficiently managing Kubernetes based environments for use within the Aeonic program. However, due to the generic way RPT is designed, it can effectively be used to manage the state of any resources that would otherwise need to be tracked and managed through confluence pages or other manual means. If this interests you, please contact Thunderbee about using RPT to manage your resources.

Currently RPT is used by the following areas:

EIC Product Staging
  • Tracking the availability of test environments for use within pipelines.
  • Automated reservation, unreservation and quarantining of environments within pipelines.
  • Automatically refresh standby environments to prevent them from becoming stale and minimizing down time of a pipeline on failure.
  • Replace unhealthy environments with fresh ones in the product staging pool to ensure they have healthy available environments
